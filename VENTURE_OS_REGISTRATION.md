## Standing positioning rule

When approaching any opportunity:
- Lead with willingness to underbid in exchange for reviews
- Immediately establish credibility with the client's specific tech stack
- Frame as a deliberate trade: senior-level delivery at below-market rate in exchange for a review
- Never apologetic, never desperate — this is a strategic choice
